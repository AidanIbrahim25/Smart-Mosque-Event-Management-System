Our History

Masjid Al Hasanah was established in 1985 to serve the growing Muslim community in Semenyih. What began as a small prayer space has now grown into a vibrant community center that offers various religious, educational, and social services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve as a center of Islamic learning and practice, fostering spiritual growth, community service, and interfaith understanding. We strive to create an inclusive environment where Muslims can connect with their faith and serve humanity.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of Islamic values and knowledge, contributing positively to society through education, charity, and community development while preserving Islamic heritage and traditions.
